About PlayAid

Entertainment at your finger tips

I believe that mental health is best maintained by having fun. PlayAid is a place where people with certain disabilities such as visual impairity or speech disparity find repose. This is also designed for those who do not have enough resources to buy their own sources of entertainment. PlayAid works using three visual hand gesture tools to create, play and show emotions. DRAW is for those children or adults who cannot buy crayons and color books. EXPRESS is a tool for those who are visually impaired and mute, to express their emotions. The MUSIC tool is for the visually impaired and those who do not have enough resources to buy themselves musical instruments.
